Hello there

I'm Charlie Duong. I am a creative thinker, creator, and life-long learner.

I use design and technology as a tool to tell a narrative. I create things that are modular with subtle animations and beautiful color palettes. I believe that design is symbiotic with usability, one cannot exist without the other.

Currently

I live and work in Amsterdam at Label A as a UX Designer.

My role is focused on solving highly complex business problems through design. As creative lead, I execute pixel perfect designs as well as inspire and challenge my team to analyze problems holistically without assumptions, think outside the box, and add value through innovation.

Previously, I worked as a Front-End Developer at openbox9 where I focused on creating designs by implementing creative solutions for user interfaces and experiences to help our clients convey their unique story. Prior to that, I worked as a Technical Analyst where I learned a great deal about product development and its best practices. I wore many hats - working on product testing, user training, and information architecture.

When my eyes aren’t glued to the screen, I enjoy nerding out about music, taking food pictures, and searching for the perfect cup of coffee.
